The Perfect Old Fashioned Cocktail

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 3 dashes bitters
  • 1 orange
  • 1 maraschino cherry
  • 1 oz water
  • 2 oz bourbon
  • Ice

Directions

  1. Add sugar to medium sized rocks glass
  2. Add bitters to soak sugar
  3. Squeeze 1 slice orange over sugar
  4. Add water (preferably warm) to sugar
  5. Wait for sugar to dissolve
  6. Fill glass halfway with ice
  7. Pour bourbon over ice
  8. Add cherry
  9. (Optional) Twist thin, small piece of orange skin over glass and drop into drink to finish
