About the Author

Dave was born in the Pacific Northwest and has enjoyed living in the area surrounding Lake Washington his entire life. While he calls Washington home, he loves to travel and does so whenever he can. He lived in the city of Seattle for ten years from 2008 until 2018, and recently moved back to Kenmore. Dave lives at home with two housemates and a little puppy-dawg named Luna.

In his free time, Dave is a member of the Columbia Choirs of Metropolitan Seattle, a local organization committed to keeping people of all ages singing throughout their entire lives!
